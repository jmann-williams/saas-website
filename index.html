Common Questions

  • Q: How does a free trial work?

    A: The free trial provides you with a full version of a small business plan for 14 days. You’ll find that the trial does impose a few temporary limits on the software, like the amount of storage you have. Otherwise, you get the full experience of the experience our software, so you know exactly what you I heard you’re getting when you become a customer.

  • Q: What happens after my free trial ends?

    A: After your trial has ended, you will automatically be downgraded to the free plan if you do nothing. If you choose to keep your paid plan, you will need to enter your payment details and confirm.

  • Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade plans at any time?

    A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at anytime. Click on the pricing tab to change your plan.

  • Q: What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

    A: If you exceed the storage limit of your plan, you will be promoted to upgrade your plan, or to contact us for more details. If you only need a little bit more, you can pay an extra $15 / 100mb.
